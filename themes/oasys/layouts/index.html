Oasys

Oasys is a free award winning app for Windows that plots and interprets serial peak expiratory flow (PEF) readings of patients suspected as having occupational asthma or work-related asthma. The computer generated score has a 94% specificity for occupational asthma diagnosis and a 75% sensitivity.
